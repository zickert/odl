PartialDerivative¶

class odl.discr.diff_ops.PartialDerivative(space, axis, method='forward', pad_mode='constant', pad_const=0)[source]¶

Bases: odl.operator.tensor_ops.PointwiseTensorFieldOperator

Calculate the discrete partial derivative along a given axis.

Calls helper function finite_diff to calculate finite difference. Preserves the shape of the underlying grid.
