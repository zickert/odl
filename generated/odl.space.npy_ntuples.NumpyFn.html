NumpyFn¶

class odl.space.npy_ntuples.NumpyFn(size, dtype='float64', **kwargs)[source]¶

Bases: odl.space.base_ntuples.FnBase, odl.space.npy_ntuples.NumpyNtuples

Vector space F^n with vector multiplication.

This space implements n-tuples of elements from a Field F, which is usually the real or complex numbers.

Its elements are represented as instances of the NumpyFnVector class.
