Notes

The functional with prior is given by:

KL based objectives are common in MLEM optimization problems and are often used as data-matching term when data noise governed by a multivariate Poisson probability distribution is significant.

The functional is related to the Kullback-Leibler cross entropy functional KullbackLeiblerCrossEntropy. The KL cross entropy is the one diescribed in this Wikipedia article, and the functional is obtained by switching place of the prior and the varialbe in the KL cross entropy functional.

For a theoretical exposition, see Csiszar1991.
